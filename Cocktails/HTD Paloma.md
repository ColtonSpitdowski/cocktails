## Ingredients
- 1 oz. [[Grapefruit Oleo Saccharum]]
- 1 oz. Lime Juice
- .75 oz. Fresh Grapefruit Juice
- Pinch of Salt
- .25 oz. Campari
- 2 oz. [[Tequila]]
- Soda Water
## Directions
1. Pour everything into cocktail shaker and add ice
2. Strain into a glass over ice
3. Top with soda water
4. Garnish with a sprig of mint

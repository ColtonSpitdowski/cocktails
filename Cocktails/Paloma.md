## Ingredients
- 1.5 oz. [[Tequila]]
- .75 oz. Lime Juice
- .5 oz. [[Simple Syrup]]
- Grapefruit Soda
## Directions
1. Pour tequila, lime juice, and simple syrup into cocktail shaker and add ice
2. Shake for 8-10 seconds
3. Double strain pour into a glass filled with ice
4. Top glass with grapefruit soda
